1. Introduction

Welcome to the Matajar Group Investor Portal. This portal helps investors submit and track their investment history details as part of the legal review and settlement processing.

Purpose of This Portal

As part of the final legal and audit process, all investors are required to verify their investment details and submit supporting documents. This information is essential for:

  • Preparing your settlement file
  • Generating your investor login credentials
  • Processing your investment claims
  • Maintaining accurate records
ℹ
Important: Please complete the form accurately to avoid delays in processing your settlement.

4. Login Process

Existing users can login with OTP verification:

1
Select Country Code
2
Enter registered Phone Number
3
Click "Send OTP"
4
Enter 6-digit OTP
5
Click "Verify & Login"

OTP Not Received?

  • Wait for timer (2 minutes), then click "Resend OTP"
  • UAE users: Check SMS inbox and phone network
  • International users: Check email inbox and spam/junk folder
  • Verify the phone number/email is correct
⚠
Security: Never share your OTP with anyone. Matajar Group staff will never ask for your OTP.

Form Completion Tips

Do's

  • Fill all required fields marked with *
  • Use accurate information from documents
  • Upload clear, readable document copies
  • Double-check before final submission
  • Save as draft if you need more time

Don'ts

  • Don't submit incomplete information
  • Don't upload blurry or unreadable files
  • Don't exceed 10MB file size limit
  • Don't submit same investment twice
  • Don't share your OTP with anyone

8. Managing Submissions

Viewing a Submission

  • Click any row in submissions table
  • Or click the View button (eye icon)
  • Download PDF copy from view page

Editing a Submission

Available for Draft and Pending only:

  • Click Edit button (pencil icon)
  • Make necessary changes
  • Save as draft or submit

Deleting a Submission

Available for Draft and Pending only:

  • Click Delete button (trash icon)
  • Confirm deletion in popup
  • Action cannot be undone
⚠
Note: Verified submissions cannot be edited or deleted.

10. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I have multiple investments. Do I need to submit separate forms?
A: Yes, please submit a separate form for each investment.
Q: What if I don't have all the documents right now?
A: You can save your form as a draft and come back later to complete it.
Q: Can I change my information after submission?
A: You can edit while status is "Pending". Once verified, changes are not allowed.
Q: How long does verification take?
A: Typically 3-5 business days. You will be notified once reviewed.
Q: What is the Court Agreement Number?
A: It's the reference number on your court document (e.g., SN2025/0000440833).
Q: Can I access the portal from my mobile phone?
A: Yes, the portal is mobile-responsive and works on all devices.
Q: Is my information secure?
A: Yes, all data is encrypted and stored securely.
